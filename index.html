How to make Chowmein ?

To prepare Maggi, first boil the required amount of water in a pan. Add the noodles and seasoning to the boiling water
and mix well.Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes until the noodles become soft. Serve the prepared Maggi hot.

Chowmein stuff

If we have a packet of Chowmein, first we open the packet and take out the noodles,
then place them on a After that,we put the noodles in a vessel and boil them for 10 minutes.
Once they are boiled,we drain them and place them in a clean bowl,then keep them aside to cool down.
